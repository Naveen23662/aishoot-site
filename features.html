Explore Our Features

AI tools that turn everyday photos into professional masterpieces.

Smart Automation

  • AI outfit detection for sarees, lehengas, kurtas, gowns and more.
  • Auto‑suggest matching backgrounds and props tailored to each outfit.
  • Batch processing for 50+ photos at once, saving hours of manual work.
  • Auto crop, align & frame photos to standard e‑commerce sizes.

Model & Pose Simulation

  • Select from virtual models with varied skin tones, hairstyles and body shapes.
  • Pose variations like standing, walking, side profiles and twirls.
  • AR try‑on lets customers see the outfit on themselves via their phone camera.

Backgrounds & Props

  • Categorized themes: Classic Studio, Traditional, Contemporary and Outdoor.
  • Seasonal themes for Diwali, Sankranti, Eid, weddings, winter and summer.
  • 3D props like chandeliers, arches, floral décor, temple backdrops and velvet drapes.
  • Realistic location simulation such as palace halls, heritage walls and ramp‑walk stages.

Image Quality & Detailing

  • Fabric‑true rendering for nets, silks, zari and embroidery.
  • HD zoom‑in crops that highlight motifs, borders and intricate detailing.
  • Lighting modes from daylight to studio to cinematic spotlights.
  • Noise, shadow and reflection adjustments for impeccable finish.

Business & Branding Tools

  • Automatic watermark or logo placement to protect your brand.
  • Export templates tailored for Instagram reels, WhatsApp catalogs and Shopify stores.
  • Before/After slider previews to visualise improvements instantly.
  • Bulk catalog export to PDF, ZIP or directly to Google Drive.

Advanced Add‑Ons

  • AI video mode converts images into reels with zoom, pan and music.
  • AI caption and hashtag generator to boost your social media reach.
  • Voice captions available in English and several regional languages.
  • Team collaboration mode connecting owners, photographers and designers.
